# Canaan Avalon Q

*Coming soon*

A high-efficiency bitcoin miner designed for HVAC duct integration.

## Overview

The Avalon Q is engineered specifically for forced-air heating applications. Unlike space heaters that radiate heat into a room, the Avalon Q is designed to connect to your home's ductwork.

## Expected Specifications

| Spec | Expected Value |
|------|----------------|
| Power | 2000-3000W |
| Heat Output | 6,800 - 10,200 BTU/h |
| Connection | Duct collar (standard sizes) |
| Control | Smart integration ready |

> **Note:** Specifications are preliminary and subject to change.

## Use Case

The Avalon Q is the mining hardware for [Kit 3: Forced Air HVAC Buddy](./kit-3.md). It integrates with your existing forced-air HVAC system to provide "first stage" heat.

## How It Differs From Mini 3

| Feature | Avalon Mini 3 | Avalon Q |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| Application | Space heating | Duct integration |
| Airflow | Open room | Enclosed duct |
| Power | 800W | 2000-3000W |
| Heat distribution | Radiant | Forced air |
