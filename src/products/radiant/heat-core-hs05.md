# Heat Core HS05

*Coming soon*

A liquid-cooled bitcoin mining system designed for hydronic heating integration.

## Overview

The Heat Core HS05 transfers heat from bitcoin mining directly to water or glycol, making it ideal for:

- Radiant floor heating
- Baseboard radiators
- Hot water pre-heating
- Pool/spa heating

## Expected Specifications

| Spec | Expected Value |
|------|----------------|
| Power | ~5000W |
| Heat Output | ~17,000 BTU/h |
| Fluid Connection | Standard plumbing fittings |
| Cooling | Liquid-cooled (no fans) |
| Noise | Near silent operation |

> **Note:** Specifications are preliminary and subject to change.

## Key Features

### Liquid Cooling

Unlike air-cooled miners, the Heat Core transfers heat directly to fluid:

- **Quiet operation** - No loud fans
- **Efficient transfer** - Direct heat exchange to water/glycol
- **Easy integration** - Standard plumbing connections

### Hydronic Compatible

Designed to integrate with standard hydronic heating components:

- Circulation pumps
- Expansion tanks
- Zone valves
- Mixing valves

## Use Case

The Heat Core is the mining hardware for [Kit 4: Radiant Boiler Buddy](./kit-4.md). It can serve as:

- **Primary heat source** - For new hydronic zones
- **First stage heat** - Paired with existing boiler
