# Canaan Avalon Mini 3

The Canaan Avalon Mini 3 is a bitcoin mining space heater designed for home use. All energy consumed by the miner is converted to heat, making it an efficient heating appliance that also earns bitcoin.

## Specifications

| Specification | Value |
|---------------|-------|
| Heating Power | 800W (~2,729 BTU/h) |
| Mining Power | Up to 40 TH/s |
| Noise Level | Under 45 dB |
| Dimensions | 30" × 4.1" × 8.5" |
| Electrical | 120V AC (standard outlet) |
| Heating Coverage | ~300 sq ft |

## Physical Design

### Air Flow
- **Intake**: Bottom of device (requires clearance)
- **Exhaust**: Front face (hot air output)

### Mounting Options
- **Feet mounts**: For placement on flat, stable surfaces
- **Wall mounts**: For wall installation (use drywall anchors or studs)

### Orientation Requirements
The Mini 3 must be level and properly oriented. The device will enter an error state and make warning sounds if tilted or improperly positioned.
