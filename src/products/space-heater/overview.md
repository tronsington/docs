# Space Heater Kits

Our space heater kits combine the Canaan Avalon Mini 3 bitcoin mining heater with an Exergy Home Assistant Hub, giving you a smart, self-hosted heating solution.

## Available Kits

### Kit 1: Smart Miner Control

The basic kit for smart control of your Avalon Mini 3.

**What's Included:**
- Canaan Avalon Mini 3 (800W heater / 40 TH/s miner)
- Exergy Home Assistant Hub
- Power supply for hub

**What You Can Do:**
- Control your miner from Home Assistant (on/off, work mode, work level)
- Build custom automations (time of use, weather-based, schedules)
- Integrate with your existing smart home devices
- Remote control via Home Assistant Cloud (optional subscription)

[Kit 1 Details →](./kit-1.md)

---

### Kit 2: Room Thermostat System

Everything in Kit 1, plus Zigbee temperature sensing for true room thermostatic control.

**What's Included:**
- Everything in Kit 1
- Zigbee antenna (GPIO hat)
- Zigbee temperature sensor

**What You Can Do:**
- Everything in Kit 1
- Accurate room temperature monitoring (not just intake air temp)
- Digital thermostat control in Home Assistant
- Automated room temperature maintenance

[Kit 2 Details →](./kit-2.md)

## Which Kit Should I Choose?

| If you... | Choose |
|-----------|--------|
| Want basic smart control and will build your own automations | Kit 1 |
| Already have a smart thermostat you want to integrate | Kit 1 |
| Want turnkey thermostatic control for a specific room | Kit 2 |
| Want the most accurate room temperature sensing | Kit 2 |
