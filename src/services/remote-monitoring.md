# Remote Monitoring

Professional monitoring and management of your hashrate heating system.

## What We Monitor

- **System Health**: Miner status, temperature, hashrate
- **Performance**: Efficiency metrics and trends
- **Alerts**: Proactive notification of issues
- **Mining Stats**: Pool performance and earnings

## Service Levels

### Basic Monitoring
- Daily system health checks
- Alert notifications
- Monthly performance reports

### Managed Service
- 24/7 monitoring
- Proactive issue resolution
- Mining pool optimization
- Firmware management
- Priority support

## How It Works

1. We configure secure remote access to your Home Assistant
2. Our monitoring systems watch for issues
3. You receive alerts for anything requiring attention
4. Managed service includes proactive intervention

## Requirements

- Home Assistant Brain (included in all kits)
- Internet connection
- Home Assistant Cloud or VPN access

## Benefits

- **Peace of Mind**: Know your system is being watched
- **Maximize Uptime**: Catch issues before they become problems
- **Optimize Performance**: Data-driven tuning recommendations
- **Expert Support**: Direct access to Exergy team
